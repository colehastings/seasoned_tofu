The Message Behind The Merch

Want to know how the logo was made? Click here.

Having merchandise with a message has always been a dream of mine since I started my personal brand. After an extensive amount of thinking, I came up with a shirt and a message that I can confidently say will change people's thinking towards a vegan diet.

So, what does this merch represent? It's all about changing the culture of the vegan community to make it more welcoming, and thereby influence more people to try a plant based diet. I want people to understand the incredible benefits of this diet, and to do that, we need to change the social stigma surrounding our culture. I promise that through this merchandise, and through our public display of it, we can convince more people to try a vegan diet. That being said, the only way we can do this is if a certain type of person wears this shirt, and does so with pride.

Do NOT plan on buying my merch if:

  • You feel the need to insult and harrass others for not being vegan.
  • You isolate yourself from everyone who isn't vegan, and dislike all humans who aren't vegan.
  • You feel that even if someone does "Meatless Mondays", they aren't worthy of being accepted.
  • You dislike those who: are on a plant based diet, but still own some products that use animal products.
  • You feel the need to debate with everyone who isn't vegan, and think you're above them.

Please consider buying my merch if:

  • You have a strong compassion towards all sentient beings.
  • You are friendly in the way that you discuss a vegan diet to those that are not.
  • You understand people's subjective realities, so you understand if they are not vegan.
  • You dislike debating but support speeches or movies about veganism.
  • You realize that someone does not have to be the "perfect vegan" (go to animal rights marches, never use plastic, exercise every day and only eat whole foods) in order to be vegan.
  • You want to see more people transition to a vegan diet.

Let's build an unbreakable, compassionate community with this merch. Let's feel optimistic and comfortable about the future of veganism when we wear this merch.
Let's show people what it really means to be vegan.
