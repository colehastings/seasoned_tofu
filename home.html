About Me

My name is Cole Hastings, and I'm a vegan bodybuilder/influencer whose mission is to help thousands around the world get in the best shape of their lives on a plant based diet. I went vegan just over a year ago, and the effects have been nothing short of spectacular. After seeing my results in the gym, I asked myself, "Why aren't more people interested in this diet?". And that's what I plan to change. I want to change the culture around veganism to make it more welcoming and friendly to the average meat and dairy eater. Veganism is about compassion towards all living creatures. I want everyone to experience this type of lifestyle, and the only way to do that is by eliminating the social stigma surrounding the vegan community. I truly believe anyone can build muscle as a vegan with the right nutrition and workout plan, and I will dispose of the myth that vegans can't build muscle as easily as those who eat animal products.
